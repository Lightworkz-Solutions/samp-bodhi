Vismaya

There's only one you, and there's only one child...

And nurturing every child's unique & innate ability, is the path to SUCCESS...

At Vismaya it's our endeavour to recognise the latent talents of the student community and help them achieve success in the domain of choice.We use Dermatoglyphics, a scientifically proven method to decode the brain's potential and talent through the physical formation of fingerprint, which has linkage to brain development. We all know no Two human have the same DNA and thus no same finger prints. Quite often people get to know their talent later in their career. Here we give an ideal option of understanding them based on their fingerprints that has answers to some questions like:

  •  Why am I not able to understand my child?
  •  I don't know if he likes what he does?
  •  Why do we have arguments over everything?

The tests easily makes us understand 3 important facets of a child and his interest.

  •  Innate Characteristic
  •  Inborn Learning Style
  •  Inborn Intelligence & Hidden Talents

Identifying them based on their interest it would be easy for both parent and the children to take up tasks that are easy for them and make it happen. This also helps discover innate abilities that quite often are not noticed due to the peer pressure that we always are under. We can understand the type of learner the student is and feed him the best way that he consumes and ultimately have less conflict with parents and children. The report is an exhaustive one that has over 50 different variables based on scientific studies and gives a great idea on what's in store for the kids and how they can prepate themselves. To have a demo please contact our counsellor and we can arrange for a demo and subsequently plan for the whole institution. This is also available for corporates in identifying talent.

Come let's get an understanding of the innate abilities of the kids and make their future a bright one!
