ABOUT US

Sunstone is a leading higer education service provider that works with academic institutions in up-skilling students for employability. Sunstone offers career-oriented training interventions for undergraduate students at 30+ institutions across 25 cities.

Sunstone's training programs are industry endorsed to enhance employability. These programs are designed for hybrid delivery with an unparalleled focus on soft skills and personality development. Sunstone's focus is to provide students with holistic educational experiences.

Become future ready with the upskilling modules of the Sunstone edge

A LITTLE HISTORY

Sunstone Education was founded in 2010 with the aim of revolutionizing the way education is delivered. It started as a small initiative by a group of passionate educators who believed in the power of personalized learning and innovative teaching methods.

As word spread about the transformative impact of Sunstone Education, the organization grew rapidly. It expanded its programs and established partnerships with leading institutions and industry experts to provide students with real-world experiences and opportunities for growth.

Looking ahead, Sunstone Education continues to push boundaries and explore new frontiers in education. With a dedicated team of educators, industry experts, and visionary leaders, Sunstone Education remains committed to empowering students to succeed in an ever-evolving world.
